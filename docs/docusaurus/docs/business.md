# Business Potential

Mantle Forge is open-source and built to stay that way. These are the natural monetization layers on top.

## Donations

The simplest path. A GitHub Sponsors button or Buy Me a Coffee link lets the Mantle/Web3 community support tooling they actually use. No infrastructure required — just a signal of real traction and a way for teams to say thanks.

## Custom Plugin Development

The `npm run plugin:<vendor>` pattern is already there. Teams with their own contract base, private network, or specific audit requirements can commission a dedicated skill pack and workflow tailored to their stack.

Each engagement produces a reusable plugin — low overhead, high leverage. Reach out via [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/BerEst12/mantle-forge/issues) to discuss a custom build.

## Premium Templates

Today there is one starter template (TokenVault). The next layer is a catalog of production-grade templates:

| Template | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| Staking contract | ERC-20 staking with rewards, emergency withdraw, and gas benchmarks |
| Lending pool | Collateral + borrow logic, interest rate model, liquidation |
| NFT launchpad | ERC-721 mint phases, allowlist, royalties |
| DAO governance | Proposal → vote → execute with timelock |

Each ships pre-configured for Mantle Sepolia with tests, gas report, and an audit checklist included. The free tier gets the basic scaffold; premium templates ship battle-tested.

## Skill Packs

The 9 skills and 16 CLIs in the repo are the free tier. Packaged skill bundles go deeper:

| Pack | Contents |
|------|----------|
| **DeFi Pack** | Merchant Moe pools, lending positions, yield farming queries |
| **Security Pack** | Deeper static analysis, invariant checks, reentrancy patterns |
| **Reporting Pack** | PDF exports, CI badges, Slack/Telegram summaries |

Teams pay for depth; the core stays open. Interested? Open a [GitHub Issue](https://github.com/BerEst12/mantle-forge/issues) with your use case.
